**Montis** is a modern ios app that keeps you informed about avalanche risks in your favorite mountain regions in Austria.
Receive personalized alerts via push notification or Telegram based on your selected areas – whether you're planning a ski trip, a tour, or just checking conditions.

Built with **React Native**, **Expo** and **Appwrite**, Montis delivers an intuitive experience and beautiful UI for mountain lovers on the go.
